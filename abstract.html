Abstract submission

Deadline

The organization invites abstracts for contributed oral presentations for the 2024 Adaptive Designs and Multiple Testing Procedures. Abstracts may be submitted online until 2 February 2024 using this form



Structure

All abstracts must be submitted online. No other forms of abstracts are permissible. Each abstract submission must include the title, list of authors and author affiliations, and abstract, with each submission having a maximum length of 2,500 characters.



Evaluation

All submitted abstracts will be reviewed by the Scientific Committee and presenting authors will be informed of the status of their abstract by the 16 February 2024.

All accepted contributed oral presentations will be assigned to a 15-minute slot within a particular scientific session. All presenting authors must have registered for the conference before the registration deadline date for their presentation to be included in the conference program.
